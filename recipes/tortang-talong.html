Tortang Talong

Description

Tortang Talong is a Filipino eggplant omelet that's simple, budget-friendly, and versatile. It can be made with just eggplant and eggs, or upgraded with onions and ground meat for extra flavor. Often paired with rice and condiments like ketchup, hot sauce, or shrimp paste, this dish is enjoyed any time of the day and reflects the Filipino knack for turning humble ingredients into something delicious.

Ingredients

  • eggplants (long and slim)
  • eggs (1-2 per eggplant)
  • diced onions
  • ground meat (optional)
  • salt and pepper
  • cooking oil

Steps

  1. Grill, boil, or bake the eggplants until soft, then peel and flatten with a fork, keeping the stems intact.
  2. Beat the eggs, seasoning with salt and pepper.
  3. Saut√© onions until golden, add ground meat (if using), and cook until done.
  4. Place an eggplant in the pan, pour beaten egg over it, top with onion-meat mixture, and cover with more egg.
  5. Cook over medium heat until set on both sides, about 2-3 minutes.
  6. Repeat with the remaining eggplants and serve with rice and your choice of condiments.
